# AlphaBreak Pricing Tiers

**Last Updated**: April 3, 2026

---

## Free ($0/mo)

Core platform access — drives user acquisition and demonstrates value.

### Market Analysis
- Sentiment analysis (8-indicator composite, 4 timeframes)
- Sector sentiment (11 sectors)
- VIX & index sentiment (SPY, QQQ, DIA)
- Commodities & crypto widget (Gold, Silver, BTC, ETH)

### Analyze Tab
- Basic price charts with common indicators (RSI, MACD, MA, Bollinger)
- Company overview (market cap, P/E, EPS, 52-week range)
- Analyst consensus + price targets (buy/hold/sell distribution)
- Ticker news feed (headlines)
- Earnings calendar (dates, estimates, surprise history)

### Trading Tools
- Trend break reports (daily, hourly, 10-min)
- Options analysis with fair value (Black-Scholes, Binomial)
- Options Greeks (Delta, Gamma, Vega, Theta)
- Forex correlations (21 pairs, USD strength)
- 13F institutional holdings (20 funds)

### Portfolio & Journal
- Portfolio tracker (paper trading, $100K)
- Trade journal (CRUD, notes, P&L tracking)
- AI trade scoring (entry/exit/timing grades)
- Shared/community journal
- Auto-import trades

### Limits
- Delayed data (15-min)
- 1 free trial per premium journal feature
- Basic watchlist (10 tickers)

## Pro ($99/mo)

Advanced analytics for active traders who want an edge.

### Everything in Free, plus:

### Analyze Tab — Advanced
- **Quant Letter Grades (A-F)** — Value, growth, profitability, momentum, revisions
- **Peer Comparison Tables** — Side-by-side P/E, EV/EBITDA, ROE vs sector peers
- **Short Interest Data** — Short float %, days to cover, squeeze risk score
- **Dividend Deep Dive** — Yield, payout ratio, growth rate, safety grade
- **Seasonality Patterns** — Monthly/weekly historical return heatmaps
- **Insider Trading Signals** — Form 4 filings, insider buy/sell activity
- **Smart Checklists** — Multi-indicator scoring rules (custom or preset)

### Options — Enhanced
- **Unusual Options Activity** — Volume vs 5-day avg, large block trades
- **Probability of Profit** — IV-based probability calculations
- **Market Maker Move** — Implied earnings move from straddle pricing

### Journal — Premium
- Tags + filters + performance by tag
- Pre-trade plans
- Post-trade reviews
- Pattern recognition
- Automated annotations
- Multi-timeframe context
- Backtesting integration

### Notifications
- Email alerts (trade signals, stop-loss, earnings)
- Custom alert rules

### Limits
- Delayed data (15-min) — real-time available in Elite
- Unlimited watchlist
- Priority support

## Elite ($299/mo)

Institutional-grade tools for serious traders and small funds.

### Everything in Pro, plus:

### Real-Time Data
- **Real-time quotes** via Polygon.io (no 15-min delay)
- **Real-time options chains** with live Greeks
- **WebSocket price streaming**

### Analyze Tab — Institutional
- **IV History + Volatility Surface** — Historical IV, term structure, skew charts
- **Auto-Detected Trendlines** — Algorithmic support/resistance, auto-fib levels
- **Supply Chain Mapping** — Revenue exposure by customer/supplier
- **3-Statement Financials** — Income, balance sheet, cash flow with trend charts
- **DuPont Decomposition** — ROE breakdown analysis
- **News NLP Sentiment** — FinBERT-scored headlines, sentiment trends

### Options — Professional
- **ORATS Historical Data** — Full IV history, earnings IV spikes, Greeks history
- **IV Crush Modeling** — Predicted post-earnings IV decline
- **Options Flow Dashboard** — Whale alerts, aggregated unusual activity

### Trading
- **Advanced Backtesting** — Strategy builder, equity curves, Sharpe/drawdown
- **Earnings Intelligence** — Surprise prediction, transcripts, key quote extraction
- **TradingView Charts** — Embedded pro charts with drawing tools

### Portfolio
- **Advanced analytics** — Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, P&L heatmap
- **Performance benchmarking** vs S&P 500, NASDAQ, sector

### Limits
- Unlimited everything
- Priority support + onboarding call
